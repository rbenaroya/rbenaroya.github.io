How It All Began

1974 - Dan, Eugene, Gary and Russell were working in the coal mines in West Virginia. It was a tough life. 16 hour days in a cold, dark, dank musty cave. They were frustrated. They needed to do something different.

No One Said It Would Be Easy

One night over some Olde English liquor the four of them decided to make a change. They wanted risk. They wanted adventure. They needed to break out. So there and then they decided to leave the coal mines and start --- EXTREME ADVENTURES. It started with a trip to Brazil. After robbing a bank (oops, did I just say that?) they headed South to the cliffs of Aribianca where they scaled and then dove off 100 meter platforms. Gary unfortunately didn't make it after hitting a rock but the three of them forged on and headed to Patagonia to ice climb on the glaciers. Russell had a bit of a slip up and fell 300 feet into the freezing ocean and could not be saved. That's okay. Today Dan and Eugene are carrying the vision forward!

Get Ready!

Today, Extreme Adventures is known as the most adrenaline rushing, death defying (sometimes) travel company in the world. We help organize trips for those who are ready to break the mold, take the risk and possibly come back alive.
